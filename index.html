About Drake

  • Aubrey Drake Graham is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, actor, and entrepreneur.

  • Drake gained recognition as an actor on the teen drama television series Degrassi: The Next Generation in the early 2000s

  • Drake released his debut studio album Thank Me Later in 2010, which debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 and was soon certified platinum

  • Among the world's best-selling music artists, with over 150 million records sold worldwide, he is ranked by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) as the world's highest-certified digital singles artist

  • Drake has also won four Grammy Awards, six American Music Awards, twenty-seven Billboard Music Awards and three Juno Awards.
